Frequently asked questions

Does VOA use AI or machine learning?

Yes! VOA uses advanced machine learning algorithms to smartly match colors, seasons, and occasions, providing you with the most personalized and stunning outfit recommendations from your wardrobe.

What weather data does the Travel Planner use?

The Travel Planner uses a single weather API to fetch real forecast data for your destination and generates packing suggestions accordingly.

Is my wardrobe data private?

Yes. Every wardrobe item, outfit, and recommendation is scoped strictly to your account.
